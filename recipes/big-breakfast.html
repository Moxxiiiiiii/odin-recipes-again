Big Breakfast

Return to Home

This breakfast spread is simple as things come, great for waking up slowly and putting together a hearty feast!

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Hash Browns (frozen)
  • Halloumi
  • Bacon
  • Butter and Olive Oil
  • Spinach leaves
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Start by heating a pan up to a low to mid temp before beginning to sizzle the bacon as well as the hashbrowns in Olive Oil.
  2. Next slice up the halloumi as well as some avocado
  3. Begin frying the Halloumi, making sure to turn everything on the pan periodically
  4. Make sure to set the toast in once you've turned everything once.
  5. Once everything is close to finishing, add the cherry tomatoes to the pan for a light colouring
  6. Once ready, serve with buttered Toast, fresh spinach and any other dressings/condiments of your choosing.
